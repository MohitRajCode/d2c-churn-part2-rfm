# Customer Retention Strategy

## Objective

The objective of this analysis is to segment customers using the RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) framework and design targeted retention strategies.

## Customer Segments

### Champions

These customers purchase frequently, spend more, and have purchased recently.

**Strategy:**

- Reward with VIP programs.
- Provide early access to new products.
- Offer exclusive discounts.

---

### Loyal Customers

These customers regularly purchase and contribute significantly to revenue.

**Strategy:**

- Introduce referral programs.
- Send personalized recommendations.
- Encourage loyalty membership upgrades.

---

### Potential Loyalists

These customers show promising engagement but need nurturing.

**Strategy:**

- Send targeted marketing campaigns.
- Offer limited-time incentives.
- Increase engagement through email campaigns.

---

### At Risk Customers

These customers have low recency, low frequency, or low spending.

**Strategy:**

- Launch win-back campaigns.
- Offer personalized discounts.
- Investigate reasons for disengagement.

## Business Impact

RFM segmentation enables the company to allocate marketing resources efficiently and improve customer retention while maximizing lifetime value.
